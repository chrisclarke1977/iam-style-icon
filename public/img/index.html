SmartWard

Smartward is a cross-platform, task management app, which improves communication and reduces human error in hospitals

Doctors will be notified, once a new patient comes to the ward. All members of staff have a dashboard view, of outstanding tasks. Be able to allocate tasks to each other, and if the junior doctors are running into trouble? They can find all doctors logged into the system,and quickly get help. With SmartWard, patients WONT be FORGOTTEN. Doctors and nurses can prioritize tasks more easily, and therefore, have more time to spend WITH their patients. Anything that helps sort the havoc that is the hospital environment, can only be for the better of patient safety.
